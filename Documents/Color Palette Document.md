* **Primary Brand Accent (Yellow)**: Used intentionally to grab attention, functioning as the single primary accent to maintain a premium feel.
    * **Figuring Out Yellow (#FFC700)**: Used for primary brand accents, CTAs, and logo highlights.
    * **Bright Gold (#FFD84D)**: Applied to hover states and micro-interactions.
    * **Burnished Gold (#E6B200)**: Reserved for active, pressed, or emphasis states.
* **Dark Foundation (Neutrals)**: Acts as the dominant canvas to create a premium, dark-first contrast.
    * **Rich Black (#0B0B0D)**: Used for primary site backgrounds, including the hero and main canvas.
    * **Charcoal (#16181D)**: Applied to elevated surfaces and cards.
    * **Graphite (#1F2228)**: Used for secondary containers and form inputs.
    * **Dark Steel (#2A2E35)**: Used strictly for borders and thin separators.
* **Typography Neutrals**: Standardized text colors ensuring readability against dark surfaces.
    * **Soft White (#F5F5F5)**: Used for primary headings and large text.
    * **Cool Gray (#B3B8C5)**: The standard for body copy and secondary text.
    * **Slate Gray (#7A818F)**: Restricted to metadata and microcopy due to lower contrast.
    * **Off White (#F7F8FA)**: Used for optional light sections or print.
* **Functional Colors**: Used minimally and exclusively for system feedback, never for branding.
    * **Emerald (#2FBF71)**: Success indicators.
    * **Amber (#F4A300)**: UI warnings and alerts.
    * **Deep Orange (#D6452D)**: Errors and destructive actions.
    * **Electric Blue (#3A86FF)**: Informational notices.
